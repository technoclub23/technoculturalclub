Techno Cultural Club aspire students to ideate future technologies and make them come true. We promote the culture of hands-on skill that develop vision and allows student to learn in community. Our Community is Led by the Faculty Coordinators - Dr. Navaid Zafar Rizvi & Dr. Vimlesh Kumar Ray. Futhermore, We have 1 General Secretary - Anushka Srivastava & 5 Secretaries - Nikhil Mishra, Dhruv Upadhyay, Ishika Singh, Rishabh, Shivi Sinha. We have a Team which is subdivided into the following categories - Management Coordinators, Coding Coordinators AI Coordinators, Developer Coordinators, Technical Coordinators, Cybersecurity Coordinators etc. The details of all have been mentioned in this page.
