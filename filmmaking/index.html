Techno Cultural Club is organising the Event - Film Making Competition for the GBU students on Saturday, 10th December 2022 in online mode. Film Making Competition is all about to showcase your Video making & editing skills.

*Video Guidelines*
All videos must adhere to the following guidelines to be eligible for the competition and judging.

1.Video length must be no less than 3 minute and no more than 5 minutes long (including title and credits, if applicable).
2.Must have a title and include at least one clear visual or audio mention of the Gautam Buddha University.
3.Must be suitable for use as promotional material for the Gautam Buddha University (no profanity, violence, defamatory statements, nudity; no invading the privacy rights of any person or entity).
4.No copyrighted material may be used, including footage, stills, animation, and music.
5.A video captions file (.srt) submitted along with your video is required.(if any)
6.1920x1080 pixel ratio preferred.

Film Making Themes

Theme for Round 1 :-
All participants have to make 3 min. Long video of our GBU. (It can be a kind of Intro-Video)

Choose a Theme for Round 2 :-
1. Your School with your perspective (for ex. ICT, SOM, etc. )
2. Unique Places of GBU Campus. (for ex. Sarovar, Meditation Centre, Library, etc.)
3. Breaking Barriers: (Cover all the Schools in a single video having video length not more than 3 min.)

* Note:- video length for round 2 can't be less than 3 and more than 5 min. Long.*
