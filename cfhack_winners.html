Project: Smart Dustbin
Description : When the dustbin is empty is shows 0% filled. The ultrasonic sensor constantly keep an eye on the level of the garbage in the dustbin and shows constantly how many percentage the dustbin is filled. when the level of garbage reachs above 90% i.e. the dustbin is about to be full the dustbin automatically send a message saying "Smart Dustbin no. 00xx is full, please collect garbage" along with a link of google maps containing exact location of the dustbin
