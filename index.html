Techno Cultural Club

With a motive to provide a common platform for Interdisciplinary technical activities of the college, Technical Club was launched in 2017. It aims to technically strengthen the students by integrating their skills in the various fields of Engineering & Technology, so as to cope up with the highly competitive environment. Many activities are conducted by the club in order to help the students explore and learn some skills which might help them in future. The club also aims competitions that are organized by other colleges, in which the students have performed exceptionally well.


About Us

Techno Cultural Club aspire students to ideate future technologies and make them come true. We promote the culture of hands-on skill that develop vision and allows student to learn in community. The community allows students to not only enhance technical skills in various field like cloud computing, drone technology, artificial intelligence & machine learning, web development, application development for various platforms, cyber security, etc. but to also learn soft skills such as team lead and team effort by promoting participation in various hackathon and events. The vision of the club is to unite technologies to serve the community. The only criteria we see is the enthusiasm and ambition of the students to learn and develop new products and technology. To create a learning environment we allow senior members to help, guide and mentor the new students.

Our Communities

Coding Community

Welcome to the coding community at Gautam Buddha University! Our community is a group of students who are passionate about coding and programming. We welcome students of all skill levels, whether you are a beginner or an experienced coder. Our community offers a variety of activities and resources to help you improve your coding skills and meet other like-minded students. We organize regular coding workshops, hackathons, and coding challenges to give you hands-on experience with different programming languages and technologies. In addition to events, we also have a dedicated online forum where members can connect and ask for help with coding problems. Our community is a great place to find support and encouragement as you learn to code. Whether you are interested in software development, data science, or web development, our coding community has something for everyone. Join us today and start exploring the world of coding!

E-Sport Community

Welcome to Gautam Buddha University's Esports Community! Our community is a hub for gamers and esports enthusiasts to connect, compete, and learn. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for gamers of all levels, from casual players to seasoned competitors. Our community hosts regular events and tournaments, both online and offline, for a variety of popular esports titles such as League of Legends, Overwatch, Dota 2, CS:GO, and many more. We also offer opportunities for players to form teams and compete in inter-university leagues and tournaments. In addition to competitions, our community also provides resources for players to improve their gameplay, such as coaching sessions, game analysis, and workshops on topics such as game strategy and team communication.

Developer Community

Are you a student who loves to code and build innovative solutions? Then look no further than our university's Developer Community! Our community is a hub for passionate developers of all levels to collaborate, learn, and grow together. Whether you're an experienced developer or just starting out, we welcome anyone with a curiosity for technology and a desire to create. As a member of our Developer Community, you'll have access to a variety of resources to help you enhance your skills and explore your interests. We offer regular workshops, coding challenges, hackathons, and networking events, all designed to give you hands-on experience and help you connect with like-minded peers. In addition, our community is supported by a dedicated team of mentors and industry professionals who are eager to share their expertise and guide you on your development journey. From web development to machine learning, we've got you covered! So what are you waiting for? Join our Developer Community today and start exploring the endless possibilities of the tech world.
